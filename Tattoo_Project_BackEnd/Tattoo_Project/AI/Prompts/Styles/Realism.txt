REALISM TATTOO STYLE GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Create a professional realism tattoo specifically designed for tattooing.

Do NOT create a digital painting.

Do NOT create a photograph.

Do NOT create concept art.

Create artwork that can realistically become a tattoo.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

Realism reproduces reality through light, shadow and texture.

The illusion of depth should come from shading rather than outlines.

The final artwork should feel believable while remaining tattooable.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Use minimal outlines.

Allow values and shadows to define form.

Avoid cartoon outlines.

Avoid thick contour lines.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

Use smooth gradients.

Create soft transitions.

Maintain clean blacks.

Avoid muddy gray areas.

==================================================
CONTRAST
==================================================

Strong blacks.

Bright highlights.

Readable midtones.

Never flatten the image.

==================================================
TEXTURES
==================================================

Create convincing fur.

Natural feathers.

Realistic stone.

Natural wood grain.

Reflective metal.

Realistic flowers.
