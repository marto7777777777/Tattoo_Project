CHICANO TATTOO STYLE GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Create authentic Chicano tattoo artwork.

Do NOT create graffiti.

Do NOT create street art.

Create elegant black and gray tattoo artwork inspired by classic Chicano tattoo culture.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

Chicano tattoos tell stories.

Emotion is more important than decoration.

Every composition should feel dramatic, meaningful and timeless.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Clean medium-weight outlines.

Refined details.

Avoid thick Traditional outlines.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

Smooth Black & Gray.

Soft transitions.

High realism without excessive texture.

==================================================
COLOR PALETTE
==================================================

Black.

Gray.

White highlights only.

No bright colors.

==================================================
COMPOSITION
==================================================

Strong central subject.

Elegant framing.

Smoke, roses and filigree should support the composition.
