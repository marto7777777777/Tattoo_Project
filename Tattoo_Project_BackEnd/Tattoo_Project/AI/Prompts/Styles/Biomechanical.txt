BIOMECHANICAL TATTOO STYLE GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Create professional Biomechanical tattoo artwork.

The tattoo should appear as a fusion between machinery and living anatomy.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

Biomechanical tattoos create the illusion that mechanical structures exist beneath the skin.

The composition should feel believable.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Technical.

Clean.

Precise.

Mechanical.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

Strong metallic contrast.

Deep shadows.

Sharp highlights.

==================================================
TEXTURES
==================================================

Steel.

Chrome.

Carbon fiber.

Pistons.

Gears.

Mechanical cables.

==================================================
COMPOSITION
==================================================

Layer mechanical parts naturally.

Create believable depth.

Maintain one continuous structure.
