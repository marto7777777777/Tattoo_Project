NEO TRADITIONAL TATTOO STYLE GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Create professional Neo Traditional tattoo artwork.

Combine classic tattoo foundations with modern illustration.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

Neo Traditional expands upon Traditional tattooing.

Maintain bold outlines.

Allow richer colors.

Allow greater detail.

Maintain tattoo readability above all.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Bold outlines.

Refined internal details.

Consistent line hierarchy.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

Smooth gradients.

Readable shadows.

Rich depth.

Avoid realism rendering.

Black

COMPOSITION

Elegant.
