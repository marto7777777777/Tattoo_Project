TRADITIONAL TATTOO STYLE GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Create authentic American Traditional tattoo artwork.

The artwork should immediately communicate classic tattoo aesthetics.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

American Traditional values simplicity.

Strong outlines.

Solid colors.

Maximum readability.

The tattoo should remain recognizable even after decades.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Extremely bold black outlines.

Consistent thickness.

No sketch lines.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

Minimal.

Simple black shading.

Avoid smooth realism.

==================================================
COLOR PALETTE
==================================================

Red

Yellow

Green

Blue

Black

Minimal skin breaks.

Avoid gradients.

==================================================
COMPOSITION
==================================================

One clear focal point.

Simple secondary elements.

Avoid unnecessary complexity.
