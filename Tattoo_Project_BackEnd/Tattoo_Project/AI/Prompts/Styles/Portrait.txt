PORTRAIT TATTOO STYLE GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Create professional portrait tattoo artwork.

The portrait should be tattooable while preserving realistic proportions and emotional expression.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

Portrait tattoos focus on expression.

The eyes are the emotional center.

Every facial feature should remain anatomically correct.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Minimal outlines.

Allow shading to define forms.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

Smooth Black & Gray transitions.

Strong contrast.

Natural highlights.

==================================================
TEXTURES
==================================================

Realistic skin.

Hair.

Beards.

Wrinkles.

Eyes.

Clothing.

Jewelry.

==================================================
COMPOSITION
==================================================

Keep the portrait dominant.

Background elements should remain secondary.

Avoid distracting details.
