ILLUSTRATIVE TATTOO STYLE GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Create professional Illustrative tattoo artwork.

Blend illustration techniques with tattoo principles.

The artwork should feel artistic while remaining tattooable.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

Illustrative tattoos combine realism, graphic design and artistic interpretation.

The artwork should express creativity without sacrificing readability.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Use expressive line weight.

Combine bold and fine lines.

Maintain clean structure.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

Use controlled gradients.

Allow artistic interpretation.

Avoid muddy rendering.

==================================================
COMPOSITION
==================================================

One clear focal point.

Decorative secondary elements.

Balanced visual hierarchy.
