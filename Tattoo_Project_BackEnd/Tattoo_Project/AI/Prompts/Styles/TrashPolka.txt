TRASH POLKA TATTOO STYLE GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Create authentic Trash Polka tattoo artwork.

Do NOT create random abstract art.

Do NOT create graphic design posters.

Create tattoo artwork inspired by the original German Trash Polka style.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

Trash Polka combines realism, abstraction, typography and graphic design.

The artwork should feel energetic, chaotic and emotional while remaining visually balanced.

Controlled chaos is essential.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Mix bold graphic lines with expressive brush strokes.

Allow imperfections where appropriate.

Never lose readability.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

High contrast black.

Minimal gray.

Use black masses to control composition.

==================================================
COLOR PALETTE
==================================================

Black

Red

White

Avoid additional colors unless explicitly requested.

==================================================
COMPOSITION
==================================================

Combine realism with abstract elements.

Mix textures.

Allow splashes, paint effects and typography.

Everything should feel intentionally chaotic.
