NEW SCHOOL TATTOO STYLE GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Create authentic New School tattoo artwork.

The tattoo should feel playful, exaggerated and full of personality.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

New School exaggerates proportions.

Bold colors.

Dynamic movement.

Expressive faces.

Creative freedom.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Bold outlines.

Variable line weight.

Expressive curves.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

Smooth.

Color driven.

High contrast.

==================================================
COLOR PALETTE
==================================================

Highly saturated colors.

Bright blues.

Purples.

Greens.

Orange.

Pink.

Yellow.

==================================================
COMPOSITION
==================================================

One dominant character.

Dynamic pose.

Decorative secondary elements.
