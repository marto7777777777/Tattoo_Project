PURPOSE

Create professional Geometric tattoo artwork.

==================================================
STYLE PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

Geometry is built on precision.

Every line should have purpose.

Every angle should feel intentional.

Symmetry should remain consistent unless asymmetry is requested.

==================================================
LINE WORK
==================================================

Perfectly clean.

Sharp.

Consistent thickness.

==================================================
SHADING
==================================================

Minimal.

Use dot shading when appropriate.

Avoid realism shading.

==================================================
COMPOSITION
==================================================

Maintain symmetry.

Maintain visual rhythm.

Avoid random shapes.
