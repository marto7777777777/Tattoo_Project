HALF ABDOMEN TATTOO DESIGN GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Design an isolated tattoo specifically intended for ONE SIDE OF THE ABDOMEN.

Never generate a human body.

The tattoo should naturally occupy one side while maintaining elegant vertical flow.

==================================================
PLACEMENT PRIORITY
==================================================

One-sided balance.

Vertical movement.

Organic flow.

==================================================
ANATOMICAL ANALYSIS
==================================================

The upper area is wider.

The lower area becomes narrower.

The tattoo should visually taper.

Avoid symmetrical full-abdomen layouts.
