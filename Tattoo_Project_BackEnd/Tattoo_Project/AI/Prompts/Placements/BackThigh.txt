InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

PURPOSE
==================================================

Design an isolated tattoo specifically intended for the BACK OF THE THIGH.

Never generate a leg.

The tattoo should fit the long muscular anatomy of the posterior thigh.

==================================================

Allow slightly wider upper compositions.

==================================================

The upper thigh provides the widest area.

The tattoo should taper toward the knee.

The composition should remain centered.
