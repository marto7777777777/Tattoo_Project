CALF TATTOO DESIGN GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

You are designing a tattoo specifically intended for the calf.

Do NOT generate a leg.

Create an isolated tattoo composition optimized for the muscular shape of the calf.

==================================================
PLACEMENT PRIORITY
==================================================

The calf supports bold vertical compositions.

The design should feel powerful.

Avoid delicate layouts that disappear visually.

==================================================
ANATOMICAL ANALYSIS
==================================================

The calf is widest in the upper third.

It gradually narrows toward the ankle.

The tattoo should visually follow this taper.

The upper section should carry more visual weight.
