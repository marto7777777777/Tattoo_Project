UPPER BACK TATTOO DESIGN GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

You are designing a tattoo specifically intended for the UPPER BACK.

Do NOT generate a back.

Create one isolated tattoo artwork optimized for the upper back.

The tattoo should immediately communicate that it belongs between the shoulders.

==================================================
PLACEMENT PRIORITY
==================================================

The upper back naturally favors broad and balanced compositions.

Width is more important than height.

Symmetry is encouraged but not mandatory.

==================================================
ANATOMICAL ANALYSIS
==================================================

The upper back is wide and almost flat.

The shoulder blades create a natural horizontal expansion.

The composition should feel stable.

Avoid tall narrow layouts.

==================================================
OVERALL SHAPE
==================================================

Preferred silhouettes

• Wide shield

• Broad wings

• Horizontal crest

• Ornamental frame

Avoid

• Tall columns

• Long spears

• Narrow forearm layouts

• Square posters

==================================================
ASPECT RATIO
==================================================

Preferred

1.6 : 1

Acceptable

1.4 : 1

to

1.9 : 1

==================================================
VISUAL FLOW
==================================================

The eye should move from the center toward both shoulders.

Maintain strong horizontal balance.

Avoid empty corners.
