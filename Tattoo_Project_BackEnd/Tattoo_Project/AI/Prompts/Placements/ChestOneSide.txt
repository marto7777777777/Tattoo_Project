UPPER CHEST TATTOO DESIGN GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Design an isolated tattoo specifically intended for ONE SIDE OF THE UPPER CHEST.

Do NOT generate a human body.

The tattoo should naturally fit the upper chest while remaining an isolated artwork.

==================================================
PLACEMENT PRIORITY
==================================================

This placement combines horizontal expansion with gentle vertical flow.

Avoid creating full chest compositions.

==================================================
ANATOMICAL ANALYSIS
==================================================

The upper chest is wide near the shoulder.

The inner edge naturally points toward the sternum.

The tattoo should visually expand outward.

Never create perfect symmetry.

==================================================
