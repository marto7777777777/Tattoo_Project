FACE TATTOO DESIGN GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Design an isolated tattoo specifically intended for FACE placement.

Never generate a human face.

Generate only the tattoo artwork.

==================================================
PLACEMENT PRIORITY
==================================================

Face tattoos must remain minimal.

Readability is more important than complexity.

==================================================
ANATOMICAL ANALYSIS
==================================================

The available area is extremely limited.

The tattoo should remain compact.

Fine details should be minimized.

• Large portraits

• Dragons

• Sleeves

• Large geometric compositions
