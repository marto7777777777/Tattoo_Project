InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

PURPOSE
==================================================

Design a tattoo specifically intended for HALF BACK placement.

Generate only an isolated tattoo artwork.

==================================================
PLACEMENT PRIORITY
==================================================

The tattoo should occupy either the left or right side while remaining visually balanced.

Avoid creating a full back composition.

==================================================
ANATOMICAL ANALYSIS
==================================================

Half back provides a tall vertical canvas.

The tattoo may expand slightly toward the shoulder.

It should naturally taper toward the lower back.
