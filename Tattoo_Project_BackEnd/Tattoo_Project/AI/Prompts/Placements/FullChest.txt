FULL CHEST TATTOO DESIGN GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Design an isolated tattoo specifically intended for the FULL CHEST.

Do NOT generate a torso.

Generate only the tattoo artwork.

The tattoo should naturally occupy the entire chest while remaining balanced and symmetrical.

==================================================
PLACEMENT PRIORITY
==================================================

The chest favors wide horizontal compositions.

The design should feel powerful, centered and stable.

==================================================
ANATOMICAL ANALYSIS
==================================================

The shoulders provide the greatest width.

The sternum forms the central axis.

The composition should expand naturally toward both shoulders.

Avoid narrow vertical layouts.

==================================================
OVERALL SHAPE
==================================================

Preferred

• Wings

• Broad shield

• Crest

• Ornamental frame

• Symmetrical floral composition

Avoid

• Tall columns

• Squares

• Poster layouts

• Equal-width rectangles

==================================================
ASPECT RATIO
==================================================

Preferred

1.7 : 1

Acceptable

1.5 : 1

to

2.0 : 1

==================================================
VISUAL FLOW
==================================================

The eye should first reach the center.

Movement should then expand evenly toward both sides.

Maintain strong visual balance.
