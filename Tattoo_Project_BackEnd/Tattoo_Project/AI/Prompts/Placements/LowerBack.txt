LOWER BACK TATTOO DESIGN GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

Design an isolated tattoo specifically intended for the LOWER BACK.

Do NOT generate a back.

Do NOT generate skin.

Create a tattoo composition that naturally follows the horizontal anatomy of the lower back.

The final artwork should immediately feel designed for this placement.

==================================================
PLACEMENT PRIORITY
==================================================

The lower back naturally favors wide and elegant compositions.

Horizontal balance is more important than vertical height.

Avoid oversized central masses.

==================================================
ANATOMICAL ANALYSIS
==================================================

The lower back is significantly wider than it is tall.

The available tattoo area forms a gentle horizontal curve.

The center provides the strongest visual anchor.

Both sides should remain visually balanced.
