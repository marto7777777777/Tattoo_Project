InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

You are designing a tattoo specifically intended for the FRONT THIGH.

Do NOT generate a leg.

Create an isolated tattoo optimized for the broad vertical surface of the thigh.

==================================================

==================================================

The thigh supports large detailed artwork.

The composition should appear powerful but elegant.

==================================================

It is broad at the top.

Gradually narrows toward the knee.

The tattoo should visually follow this transition.
