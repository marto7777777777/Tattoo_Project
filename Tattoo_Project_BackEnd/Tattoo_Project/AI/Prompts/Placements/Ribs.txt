RIBS TATTOO DESIGN GUIDE
InkRoute AI Knowledge Base

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

You are designing a tattoo specifically intended for the human ribs.

Do NOT generate ribs.

Do NOT generate a torso.

Create an isolated tattoo artwork that naturally follows the long curved anatomy of the rib cage.

The artwork should immediately appear suitable for the ribs because of its proportions and movement.

==================================================
PLACEMENT PRIORITY
==================================================

The ribs prioritize movement over symmetry.

Flow is more important than density.

Elegance is more important than complexity.

==================================================
ANATOMICAL ANALYSIS
==================================================

The rib cage is long and naturally curved.

The tattoo should suggest this curvature through composition.

Never create a rigid straight layout.

The upper section may be slightly wider.

The lower section should naturally taper.

==================================================
OVERALL SHAPE
==================================================

Preferred silhouettes

• Flowing ribbon

• Curved blade

• Organic vine

• Elegant spear

• Long botanical composition

Avoid

• Squares

• Wide horizontal layouts

• Circular badges

• Heavy rectangular blocks

VISUAL FLOW
==================================================

Movement should travel diagonally or vertically.

The eye should naturally follow one continuous path.

Never divide the tattoo into disconnected areas.
